Webhook & CRM Technical Notes

Calendly Hidden Fields: lead_id, rep_id, appt_type (office|virtual)

On invitee.created webhook:
‚Ä¢ Match to lead (by lead_id or email)
‚Ä¢ Set appropriate Disposition
‚Ä¢ Create Appointment object in CRM
‚Ä¢ Notify rep (email/Slack)
‚Ä¢ PAUSE nurture sequence immediately

On reschedule/cancel webhook:
‚Ä¢ Update Appointment record
‚Ä¢ If cancel: Set disposition to "Appointment Canceled"
‚Ä¢ Trigger short Re-book Micro-Flow (1‚Äì2 emails) unless already rebooked

Shopify & QuickBooks Integration Points

1. Real-Time Order Monitor (All Stages):
‚Ä¢ Shopify webhook: order.created - Immediately pause nurture, update to "Customer - Order Placed", exit to customer journey
‚Ä¢ QuickBooks webhook: invoice.created - Same actions as above
‚Ä¢ This monitor runs parallel to entire nurture sequence

2. Post-Appointment Verification:
‚Ä¢ Trigger: When appointment status = "Complete"
‚Ä¢ Check Shopify orders by email/phone
‚Ä¢ Check QuickBooks invoices by email/phone
‚Ä¢ If order found: Move to customer journey
‚Ä¢ If no order: Create sales follow-up task for rep

3. Pre-Anna Email Verification (Before Stage 9 & 10):
‚Ä¢ Before sending 30-day and 60-day Anna emails
‚Ä¢ Re-verify prospect status against Shopify/QB
‚Ä¢ Prevents reaching out to contacts who became customers outside CRM

4. Weekly Batch Verification:
‚Ä¢ Schedule: Every Monday 6:00 AM
‚Ä¢ Pull all contacts with disposition = "In Nurture"
‚Ä¢ Bulk check against Shopify orders + QB invoices
‚Ä¢ Auto-remove any matches, tag as "Customer - Non-CRM Order"
‚Ä¢ Catches orders placed via phone, trade show, direct contact

5. QuickBooks Payment Trigger:
‚Ä¢ Webhook: payment.received
‚Ä¢ Match contact by email/phone
‚Ä¢ Immediate action: Remove from nurture, update to "Active Customer", notify customer success team

Data Matching Priority:
1. Email (primary)
2. Phone number (secondary)
3. Full name + company (tertiary fallback)
Always log match method for audit trail
