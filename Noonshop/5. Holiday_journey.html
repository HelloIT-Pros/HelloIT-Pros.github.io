CRM Technical Notes

Target Audience: ALL contacts in CRM database

Includes:
• Prospects (in nurture)
• Leads (not yet customers)
• First-time customers
• Active customers
• Inactive customers
• Dormant customers
• Win-back journey contacts

Excludes:
• Unsubscribed contacts
• "Do Not Contact" tags
• Invalid email addresses

Timing Logic:
• System maintains holiday date calendar (updated annually)
• Campaign triggers 3-5 days before each holiday
• Send time: 9:00 AM in recipient's timezone (if available, else 9:00 AM EST)
• One email per holiday maximum

Personalization Fields Required:
• First Name
• Practice Name (if customer/known)
• Assigned Rep Name
• Rep email signature

Design Notes:
• Plain text or minimal HTML (looks handwritten)
• Rep's email signature at bottom
• NO images, NO CTAs, NO links
• Mobile-friendly formatting

Holiday Calendar & Automation

Annual Holiday Schedule (In Chronological Order - 19 Total Touchpoints):
1. New Year: December 28-30 (send before New Year's Eve)
2. Valentine's Day: February 14 (send February 12)
3. Daylight Saving - Spring: 2nd Sunday in March (send Friday before)
4. Save Your Vision Month: March (send March 1)
5. Memorial Day: Last Monday in May (send Thursday before)
6. First Day of Summer: June 21 (send June 20)
7. National Sunglasses Day: June 27 (send June 26)
8. July 4th: July 4 (send July 2)
9. National Eye Exam Month: August (send August 1)
10. National Relaxation Day: August 15 (send August 15)
11. Labor Day: First Monday in September (send Thursday before)
12. National Read a Book Day: September 6 (send September 6)
13. First Day of Fall: September 22 (send September 22)
14. National Coffee Day: September 29 (send September 29)
15. World Sight Day: 2nd Thursday in October (send that day)
16. Halloween: October 31 (send October 30)
17. Daylight Saving - Fall: 1st Sunday in November (send Friday before)
18. Thanksgiving: 4th Thursday in November (send Tuesday before)
19. Christmas/Holidays: December 20-23 (send once mid-December)

No Overlap with Other Campaigns:
• Holiday emails do NOT pause other journeys
• Holiday emails run in parallel to all active campaigns
• Everyone gets holiday greetings regardless of journey status

Rep Assignment Logic:
• If contact has assigned rep → Email from that rep
• If contact has no assigned rep → Email from Anna or default rep
• Ensure rep name personalization works for all scenarios

Key Principle:
These are pure relationship touches. Zero sales intent. Just genuine human connection. This builds trust and goodwill that pays dividends year-round.
