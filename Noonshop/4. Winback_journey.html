CRM Technical Notes

Entry Trigger: Customer tagged as "Inactive Customer"

Entry Criteria:
• No order in 90+ days from last purchase, OR
• First-time customer: Expected Reorder Date + 72 days with no second order
• System auto-tags as "Inactive Customer - Win-Back"

Personalization Requirements:
• All rep emails use conversational, handwritten style (no corporate speak)
• Product recommendations based on purchase history
• Practice name used throughout messaging
• Rep includes personal notes/observations when possible

Status Updates:
• Entry: Inactive Customer - Win-Back
• On order: Active Customer → Exit to Active Journey
• After Stage 13 (no order): Dormant Customer → Long-term nurture

Shopify Integration Points

1. Real-Time Order Monitor (All Stages):
• Shopify webhook: order.created
• Match to customer email OR phone
• If customer in Win-Back Journey → Immediately exit to Active Customer Journey
• Update status to "Active Customer"
• Send rep notification: "Great news! [Customer] just reordered!"

2. Purchase History Analysis:
• Pull past order data for personalized product recommendations in Stage 5
• Identify favorite styles, price points, quantities
• Rep reviews before sending Stage 5 to add personal touch

3. Rep Notification System:
• When customer in Win-Back places order, send immediate notification to rep
• Email subject: "Great news! [Customer Name] just reordered!"
• Include order details and suggest personal follow-up call

Data Matching Priority:
1. Customer ID (primary)
2. Email address (secondary)
3. Phone number (tertiary)
Always log match method for audit trail

Active Integration Summary:
• Real-Time Order Monitor: Shopify webhooks monitoring all stages for reactivation
• Purchase History Engine: Personalized product recommendations
• Rep Notification System: Alert reps when win-back succeeds
